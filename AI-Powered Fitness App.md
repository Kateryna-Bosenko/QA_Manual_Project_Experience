5. Reported and tracked defects in Trello
6. Verified bug fixes and performed re-testing
#### Bug Reporting
* 30+ bugs identified and documented
* Bug severity ranged from Trivial to Critical
##### Issues included:
* Registration accepting invalid data
* Login possible without email verification
* Training plan generation freezes
* Incorrect calorie and workout time calculations
* Progress data not updating after completed weeks
* Broken Logout and Settings functionality
* UI layout issues (misaligned elements, missing borders, scroll artifacts)
* Multiple clicks required for actions (Save / Finish)
* Email & password change not saving
* Password recovery email not sent
#### Each bug report included:
Clear summary; Steps to reproduce; Expected vs actual result; Environment details; Screenshots / video attachments;
